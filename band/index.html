Steve Chab Band

Deceptively technical songwriting pairs with obsessively intentional lyricism. Pittsburgh's Steve Chab Band distill life's finest moments into a rich blitz of emotion. Hear terse poems sung from Steve's hand-made chapbook. Thumb through this intimate experience at their live performances, summoning heart to sleeve. Unearth the lost, dissonant beauty of 90s-tinted emo. Every earful of this luscious trio sway you with intricate rhythms and roaring guitar. Sit back, marinate, and rouse your spirit with SCB's meticulously-crafted, literary music.

Personnel

Steve Chab on guitar/vox, Glenn Durham on drums, Alex Williams on bass.
